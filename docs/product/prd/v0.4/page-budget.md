## Current State

A PeriodSelector controls the active pay period and view mode (monthly / biweekly / etc). A BudgetSummaryBar shows total prorated vs actual income and expenses. Budget lines are grouped by category into Expenses and Income sections, each rendered as BudgetLineGroup components containing BudgetLineRow items. An AddBudgetLineDialog handles creation. Stale/offline states are surfaced via banners. A cached offline fallback shows the last-known budget lines.
