## Current State

Header shows net worth with multi-currency support and a stale-rate warning. An "Add Account" button opens the AccountForm dialog. A filter/sort toolbar supports filtering by type, institution, and group (assets / liabilities) and sorting by various keys. Accounts are rendered as cards in a grid, grouped into "My Accounts" and "Shared with Me" sections. Archived accounts appear in a collapsible section. Each card has Edit, Archive/Restore, and Share (ManageSharesDialog) actions.
